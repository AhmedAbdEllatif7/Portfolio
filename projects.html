School Management

The School Management System improves communication in educational institutions. It helps manage student information, teacher schedules, and facilitates parent-teacher interactions.

Features:

  • Efficient student information management
  • Supports multiple languages (en, ar)
  • Supports multiple user types, such as administrators, students, teachers, and parents
  • Distinct permissions and privileges for each user role
  • Administrators can easily upload important documents, circulars, newsletters, and other files for Students, Teachers, and Parents.
  • Administrators can define various types of fees, including tuition fees, bus fees, and more.
  • allows teachers to add quizzes. Students entering these quizzes and can view their results.
  • Teachers can manage quiz details, such as the quiz name, subject, grade, classroom, and section.
